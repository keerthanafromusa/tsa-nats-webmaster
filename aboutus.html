About Us

Planting the Seed

2017-2021

We began our journey as two chefs fresh out of culinary school, with a shared dream: to bring the warmth, richness, and complexity of Moroccan cuisine to our community with a modern twist of emphasizing sustainable, plant-based foods. We spent these years being curious, creative, and practical by laying the foundation. We set out to create something meaningful—Sufra.

Laying the Groundwork

2021-2022

With the vision of Sufra coming to life, we began to make tremendous progress towards establishing our location. We worked closely with Moroccan artisans and designers to ensure the space reflected the soul of Morocco: earthy colors, vibrant tiles, and a welcoming environment. We launched Sufra and spread the word in hopes of inviting the community to join us on our journey.

Bringing Sufra to Life

2022-2023

Once Sufra’s doors opened, our dreams turned into a reality. We began to host exciting launch events and community gatherings to showcase the beauty of Moroccan culture and cuisine. Every dish served told a story. Every guest served became a part of our Sufra family. And in many of these moments, it wasn’t just about the food; it was about connecting us through our shared love for Sufra.

Where We Are Now

2023-Present

Today, we continue to flourish. We host seasonal events and partner with local artists and sustainable brands. We continue to welcome our guests like family. We stick by our mission and continue to carry sustainability and community throughout our restaurant.

Our Mission

At Sufra, we offer a plant-based, sustainable journey through bold flavors and modern takes. Our mission is to treat every guest like family and create a welcoming space to gather and feast. We emphasize the use of sourcing fresh produce from local farms and ensuring that every dish is both flavorful as well as sustainable. Every ingredient is carefully chosen to honor the community we are a part of and create a feast our Sufra family will enjoy!

Meet The Chefs

Keerthana Karthikeyan

Keerthana Karthikeyan is our Head Chef. She is a graduate from one of the top culinary schools in the world, The Culinary Institute of America. She is an expert in baking, particularly pastries, and is responsible for all kitchen operations.

Niharika Nagendran

Niharika Nagendran is our Executive Chef. She is a graduate from one of the top culinary schools in the world, Le Cordon Bleu. She oversees all kitchens and the different aspects of Sufra’s culinary operations. She specializes in food from Middle Eastern and African cuisines.
